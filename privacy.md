# **Privacy Policy**

**Effective Date:** April 23, 2026  
This Privacy Policy describes how Matteo Da Ros ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with the HikeTracker mobile application (the "App"). This policy is governed by the laws of the Province of Ontario and the federal laws of Canada applicable therein, including the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

## **1\. No Collection of Personal Information**

We believe in your right to privacy. The App is designed to function without requiring a user account, registration, or sign-in.

* We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII).  
* We do not have access to your name, email address, phone number, or contacts.  
* We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or any third-party tracking libraries.

## **2\. Location Data**

The App's core functionality requires access to your device's GPS location to record your hiking path and provide navigation back to your starting point.

* **Local Processing Only:** All location data is processed entirely on your device.  
* **No Transmission:** Your coordinates and hiking routes are never uploaded to our servers or shared with any third party by us.  
* **Storage:** Recorded paths are stored in the App's local database on your device. No copies exist on any server we operate.  
* **Retention:** We retain no copies of your data. All locally stored data is permanently removed when you clear the App's data or uninstall it.

The App requests the following Android permissions:

* ACCESS\_FINE\_LOCATION — to record your GPS position during a hike  
* ACCESS\_COARSE\_LOCATION — as a fallback when fine location is unavailable  
* FOREGROUND\_SERVICE — to keep location recording active while the App is running in the background  
* POST\_NOTIFICATIONS — to display a persistent status bar notification while a hike is actively being recorded in the background, ensuring you are always aware the App is running

## **3\. Third-Party Services — Map Data (OpenStreetMap)**

To provide map visualisations when an internet connection is available, the App downloads map tiles from OpenStreetMap servers via the osmdroid library.

* During this process, your IP address and basic device/request metadata may be visible to OpenStreetMap's servers as part of standard HTTP communication. We have no control over this.  
* This data is governed solely by the OpenStreetMap Foundation Privacy Policy.  
* We do not receive, access, or store any information from these tile requests.  
* **Local Caching:** To improve performance and reduce internet data usage, downloaded map tiles are cached locally on your device. This cached data contains no personal information and is deleted if you clear the App's storage or uninstall the App.

## **4\. App Distribution — Google Play**

The App is distributed via Google Play. By downloading and using the App, you may also be subject to Google's data practices, which are independent of this policy and governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

* Google Play may collect installation and usage telemetry as part of its platform services.  
* Android's crash reporting system (Android Vitals) may automatically transmit crash and ANR (App Not Responding) reports to Google, which are visible to us in aggregate form in the Play Console. These reports do not contain your location data or any information we collect. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

## **5\. Internet Access**

The App uses internet access solely to download map tile data from OpenStreetMap. It does not use the internet to transmit your location, activity, usage statistics, or any other data to us.

## **6\. Data Security**

Because we do not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no data held by us that could be subject to a breach on our end. The security of data stored locally on your device depends on your device's own security settings and operating system.

## **7\. Children's Privacy**
